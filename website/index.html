Who We Are

About ARXN

Apogee Research-X Nexus Pvt. Ltd. is a Delhi-based deep-tech research company founded in August 2025, operating at the intersection of photonics and AI-driven fabrication.

We lead cutting-edge research activities in AI and photonic IC fabrication while preparing technical research proposals for government funding in photonic IC fabrication and topological device research. Our multidisciplinary approach bridges fundamental physics with practical device engineering.

Beyond research, we are deeply committed to engineering education — mentoring students preparing for India's most competitive examinations and publishing high-quality revision content through our blog platform.

Our Mission

To advance India's semiconductor photonics ecosystem through world-class research, strategic government partnerships, and the cultivation of a new generation of electronics engineers equipped to innovate at the nanoscale.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized nexus of photonic and AI research — bridging academia, industry, and policy to accelerate technology transfer from lab to fab.

Research Verticals

Our research spans three interconnected verticals — each pushing the boundaries of what's possible in photonics, AI, and integrated device engineering.

Photonic IC Fabrication

Priority Focus

Topological Devices

AI-Driven Research
